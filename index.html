NYC Cultural Organizations

This is an interactive map of cultural organizations in New York City, sourced from the NYC Department of Cultural Affairs (DCLA).

I study organizations and am interested in how they are classified, including what gets classified as an organization. Looking at their website, there is a list of organizations that provide services, but no map or guide. So I wanted to explore the data spatially, rendering a prototype map.

Since this is a minimum viable product approach final project, I can't go into the level of detail I would like to. But I may examine further over time.
